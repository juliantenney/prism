# Learner-Time Allocation Framework

Working heuristic ranges for a 60-minute page (to test, not final universal rules):

- Core reading: approximately 20-35 minutes maximum depending on level and activity mix
- Active work (thinking/writing/application/discussion): approximately 20-35 minutes
- Transitions/orientation/reflection: approximately 5-10 minutes

## Notes

- These ranges are assumptions, not standards.
- Different learner levels/contexts may require different allocations.
- The framework is intended to guide controlled experiments in Sprint 57A.
