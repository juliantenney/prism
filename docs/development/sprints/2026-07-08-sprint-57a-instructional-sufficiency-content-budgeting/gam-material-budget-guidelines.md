# GAM Material Budget Guidelines

GAM should generate sufficient materials, not maximal materials.

## Guidance

- Favor concise, purposeful materials aligned to required activities.
- Ensure materials are sufficient for activity completion.
- Avoid textbook-like exposition unless activity requirements genuinely require it.
- Preserve clear linkage between required materials and produced materials.

## Anti-pattern

Using excessive explanatory volume as a substitute for instructional fit or activity feasibility.
