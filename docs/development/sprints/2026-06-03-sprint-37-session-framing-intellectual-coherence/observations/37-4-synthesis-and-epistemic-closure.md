## Executive summary

**CI and Marx fixtures already achieve partial epistemic closure** via ### Closure / ### Debrief bullets and `study_tips` that ask what changed, what was hardest to justify, and what evidence would strengthen conclusions.

**Gaps:** **Climate** ends without page-level synthesis section; **RNA** ends with **procedural revision** (`re-read summary`, `use rationales after answering`) not clarified understanding; CI `study_tips` mix **study-skills** bullets with epistemic bullets without always naming **what distinction is now clearer**.

**37-4 fix:** **Epistemic synthesis and closure (auto-applied)** block ties endings to **37-2 tensions** and **37-3 arcs** using four closure principles — without reopening Sprint 35 activity-level judgement patterns.

## Epistemic vs procedural endings

| Ending type | Signals | Example (RNA `support_notes`) |
|-------------|---------|-------------------------------|
| **Procedural** | Task done, how to revise, mechanics | Re-read summary; use rationales after answering |
| **Recap** | Topics listed | “This page covered intervals and p-values” |
| **Epistemic** | Clearer distinction, judgement possible, misconception less persuasive | Procedure vs interval move now governs overlap claims |

**Target ratio for `study_tips`:** ≥1 epistemic synthesis bullet; ≤1 optional technique bullet; 2–4 bullets total.

## Closure principles (37-4 vocabulary)

Use at least **one** per page-level or final-activity closure (concise):

| Principle | Question shape |
|-----------|----------------|
| **What should now be clearer** | Which distinction or concept pair is now stable? |
| **What distinction can now be sustained** | What merge error should feel less persuasive? |
| **What kind of judgement is now possible** | What verdict can you defend with evidence rules? |
| **What explanatory move matters differently** | Which reasoning move now governs later tasks? |

Pair with **37-2 tension** (name the distinction) and **37-3 arc** (name the final phase: judge / transfer).

## Failed closure rhetoric (reject)

| Pattern | Why reject |
|---------|------------|
| “You have completed…” | Procedural only |
| “Reflect on your learning” / diary tone | Non-epistemic |
| Generic recap paragraph | Summary not synthesis |
| Key takeaways bullet dump (>4) | Bloat |
| Detached revision tips without epistemic link | RNA pattern |
| Motivational / therapeutic close | Non-academic |
| “Well done” / “Great job” | Non-epistemic |
| Re-opening full overview | Duplication |
