\# Interpretation



The framework begins with the PRISM North Star.



The North Star is realised through four learner transformations:



\* Understanding

\* Capability

\* Judgement

\* Independence



Design principles guide how those transformations are developed.



Developmental pathways describe how growth typically occurs.



Activity types are manifestations of educational intent.



Learning experiences are the final realisation of the framework.



Metacognition operates across the entire framework.



Learning success is an indicator that meaningful learner development is occurring.



\---



\# Core Principle



PRISM optimises for learner development.



Content, activities, assessment, feedback and technology are valuable only insofar as they contribute to understanding, capability, judgement and independence.
