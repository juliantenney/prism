Facilitator run sheet — workshop scenarios

Audience: Year 2 nursing students

Overview

A facilitated workshop applying observation and escalation judgement to simulated ward scenarios.

Learning Activity

Scenario walkthrough

Duration: 25 mins

Task: Apply structured observation before escalation.

Guidance

  • Work in pairs. Complete the checklist for each scenario before discussing escalation.

Materials

Scenario 1: Scenario A

Patient reports increasing breathlessness; observations borderline.

Checklist

  • ☐Confirm patient identity
  • ☐Note vital signs trend
  • ☐Identify escalation trigger

Output

Completed checklist plus one escalation rationale.

Support note: Facilitator circulates during minutes 10–20.

Formative Assessment Check

Question 1

Which observation most strongly supports immediate escalation?

  • A. Stable saturation
  • B. Rising respiratory rate with distress
  • C. Mild headache
