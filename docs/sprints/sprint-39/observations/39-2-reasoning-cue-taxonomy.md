## Candidate cue types (explore)

| Cue type | Definition (draft) | Example reasoning move | Distinct from |
|----------|-------------------|------------------------|---------------|
| **discriminating cue** | Feature that separates two confusable categories | CPI vs deflator coverage | topic label |
| **comparison cue** | Dimension or contrast learners must hold simultaneously | aim × audience (Marx) | decorative columns |
| **mechanism cue** | Directed relationship learners must trace | radiative imbalance arrow | topic diagram |
| **evidence cue** | For/against structure or weight signal | template line strength | copied evidence list |
| **boundary cue** | Limit of claim or scope | “cannot infer significance from overlap alone” | verdict text |
| **quantitative cue** | Exact endpoint, interval, or unit relationship | CI segment endpoints | invented numbers |
| **trade-off cue** | Competing explanation or policy vs mechanism | mechanism vs policy transfer limit | single-cause poster |
| **evaluation cue** | Criterion for weighing evidence | strength distinction in t-chart | pre-weighted scale |
| **procedural cue** | Step order or operation move | CI procedure vs interpretation | worked example duplicate |
