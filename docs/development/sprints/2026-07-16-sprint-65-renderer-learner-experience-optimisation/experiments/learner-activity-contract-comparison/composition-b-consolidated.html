Marx A3 — Comparing Marx's Major Works (rich framing)

Comparing Marx's Major Works

20 mins

Why this activity

As you compare these texts, notice how Marx's political and economic arguments differ.

Also: Build on the cause–effect links from the previous activity when judging audience and purpose.

Your task

Complete the comparison table using the prompts.

How to approach it

Focus on purpose, audience, and argument style—not only topic overlap.

More guidance
  • Disciplinary lens: Thinking as a historian comparing texts
  • Evidence: Cite a phrase from each excerpt when you fill the comparison table.
  • Structure: Claim → evidence from the text → implication for the reader.
  • Distinction: Contrast revolutionary programme with systematic critique.

Support

Try it — Comparison prompts

  • What is the purpose of each work?
  • Who is the audience?

Reflect

After comparing, explain in one sentence which text would persuade workers more effectively and why.
