## Intent

Provide learner-facing **exposition that connects multiple ideas in substantive prose** — building conceptual linkage before practice — with **integrative relational explanation** and, for strong realisation, **intellectual progression** and an **applied illustration** that does not pre-answer the learner's independent task.

---

## Instructional Problem

The `text` material teaches or frames; it must not be a label, specification synopsis, definition table without connective prose, or duplicate of the activity task instruction. Sprint 44 sentinel evaluation (Pass 1) and inter-rater validation tested exposition on Activity A1 in both domains.

Evidence:

- **Marx M1** — **Strong** in Pass 1 and Inter-Rater Validation (unanimous). Multiple concepts defined in linked prose; integrative applied example (delivery worker); distinct from A1 task assignment.
- **Photosynthesis M1** — **verdict split**: Pass 1 **Minimum**; Inter-Rater **Strong**. Exposition block links energy and matter transformations and explains equation components; **cognition-cue block appended after exposition** (FM-07). Pass 1 withheld Strong for definitional gloss without integrated applied scenario; Inter-Rater awarded Strong on exposition body while recording FM-07 contamination.

The instructional problem SP-01 addresses:

- **FM-07** — cognition-cue collapse: upstream orientation fields (self-explanation, transfer, misconception, revision triggers) materialised inside the `text` GAM body, violating one move per material (Photosynthesis M1, M2, M4 in evaluation passes).
- **FM-08** (Pass 1 only) — exposition without applied integrative bridge: Photosynthesis M1 equation gloss passed minimum but lacked the applied closing example that elevated Marx M1 in Pass 1.

Neither M1 body **Failed**. Both meet §5.1 minimum on the exposition block proper. SP-01 documents what separates unanimous Strong (Marx M1) from disputed Strong/Minimum (Photosynthesis M1) without resolving evaluator disagreement on applied-bridge weighting.

---

## Minimum Realisation Shape

Minimum structure evidenced on **both** Marx M1 and Photosynthesis M1 exposition blocks (both passes scored at least Minimum on instructional function):

1. **Substantive prose** — not a label, synopsis, or specification restatement. Both materials teach through multi-sentence explanations.
2. **At least two distinct ideas linked in explicit prose** — §5.1 minimum:
   - Marx M1: capitalism, surplus value, class struggle, alienation — relational links (`difference between…`, `leads to`, class interests).
   - Photosynthesis M1: energy transformation and matter transformation explicitly linked; equation components glossed and connected to process.
3. **Learner-facing tone appropriate to higher education** — definitional and explanatory, not facilitator-only notes.
4. **Distinct from activity task instruction** — teaches concepts for A1; does not assign the learner's write-an-explanation task as the dominant mode of the exposition block.
5. **Coherent progression within the exposition block** — Marx: concept sequence building to example; Photosynthesis: process statement → equation → component meanings → key integrative insight.

**Minimum does not require** (evidenced as absent or disputed):

- Integrated applied scenario closing the exposition (Pass 1: absent on Photosynthesis M1; Inter-Rater Strong without it).
- Cognition-cue-free body (Photosynthesis M1 fails this; still Minimum/Strong on exposition).
- Evaluative criteria exposition (not applicable to A1 definitional arc; Marx M12 is separate `text` on evaluate arc — out of SP-01 benchmark pair).

## Strong Realisation Shape

### Evidenced on Marx M1 — unanimous Strong (Pass 1, Inter-Rater)

1. **Clear intellectual progression** — concepts introduced in sequence (system → surplus value → class struggle → alienation) before synthesis.
2. **Explicit relational language between ideas** — surplus value linked to profit and exploitation; class struggle linked to opposing interests; alienation linked to loss of control and meaning.
3. **Concepts connected to practice context** — Pass 1: concepts align with A1 `expected_output` (apply ≥3 concepts to a scenario). Learner can see what ideas will be used for without receiving the independent scenario answer.
4. **Applied integrative illustration** — closing **Example** paragraph (delivery worker, platform, surplus value, class relationship) bridges concepts to a concrete case **without pre-answering** the learner's factory/new-scenario deliverable (worked example uses factory; exposition example uses delivery worker).
5. **No cognition-cue contamination** — exposition body ends before next material; no appended orientation metadata in Marx M1.
6. **Connective prose, not definition table only** — concepts explained in sentences with relationships, not isolated glossary cells.

Pass 1: "Intellectual progression; concepts linked to applied reasoning; integrated applied example."  
Inter-Rater: "Substantive prose linking multiple ideas; clear progression (equation → components → integrative insight)" on Marx; same Strong on relational criteria.

### Evidenced on Photosynthesis M1 exposition block — disputed Strong

**Features present (both passes acknowledged on exposition proper):**

7. **Integrative key insight** — `Photosynthesis is both: an energy transformation … a matter transformation`; conservation principle (`does not create matter or energy`).
8. **Equation operationalised** — component gloss links inputs/outputs to roles (atmosphere, roots, chlorophyll, bonds, by-product).
9. **Multi-idea linkage** — energy and matter frames explicitly connected.

**Pass 1 withheld Strong because:**

- Exposition "largely definitional/equation-gloss without an integrated applied scenario bridging to the learner's independent explanation."
- Cognition-cue block appended (FM-07) — weakened strong realisation; did not fail minimum on exposition body.

**Inter-Rater awarded Strong because:**

- Exposition performs connective teaching; progression equation → components → key insight; FM-07 noted as format pollution not function failure.

### Not evidenced as required for Strong in this pattern entry

- Three+ operational evaluative criteria (evaluate-arc `text` — Marx M12, not M1).
- `Use this when…` procedure cues — §5.8 strong signal; not on M1 bodies.
- Possessive own-context framing — transfer move; belongs in `transfer_prompt` (SP-03), not exposition.

### Suggested strong shape (M1 unanimous features + Pass 1 discriminator)

10. **Applied illustration closing exposition** — Marx M1 model; Pass 1 differentiator vs Photosynthesis M1; Inter-Rater did not require for Photosynthesis Strong.

## Detection Signals

### Minimum

Reviewer can confirm (both M1 exposition blocks):

- [ ] Substantive teaching prose (not title-only or synopsis).
- [ ] ≥2 ideas linked with explicit relational language.
- [ ] Learner-facing HE tone.
- [ ] Teaches/framing distinct from assigning the learner task.
- [ ] Reviewer can answer "What two ideas does this connect?" from the body alone.

### Strong

Reviewer can confirm — **M1 unanimous signals (Marx)** plus **optional applied illustration**:

- [ ] Intellectual progression within the block.
- [ ] Relational/explanatory links between concepts (not inventory only).
- [ ] Concepts orient toward activity `expected_output` without pre-answering deliverable.
- [ ] Applied integrative example or scenario illustration (Marx M1 — Pass 1; disputed requirement for Strong on Photosynthesis M1).
- [ ] Exposition body free of cognition-cue / orientation metadata appendages (FM-07).
- [ ] **Declare calibration stance** on Photosynthesis M1 if scoring Strong without applied bridge.

### Weak

Below strong; may still be Minimum:

- Equation or definition gloss with thin connective insight (Pass 1 Photosynthesis pattern).
- Cognition-cue block appended after exposition (FM-07 — Photosynthesis M1).
- Component list without integrative relational paragraph.
- Exposition that reads as glossary fragments without progression.

### Failed

Not evidenced on M1. §5.1 Failed would apply to empty body, synopsis-only, or task-duplication dominant — not observed in sentinel set.

## Authoring Guidance

Evidence-backed guidance only.

1. **Write substantive connective prose** — link ≥2 ideas with explicit relational language (`difference between`, `leads to`, `both X and Y`, `rearranges and converts`).

2. **Build intellectual progression** — move from process statement or core concept through supporting ideas to integrative insight (Photosynthesis key insight; Marx concept chain to example).

3. **Operationalise symbols or terms in prose** — Photosynthesis equation components; Marx concept definitions with application meaning, not labels alone.

4. **Add an applied integrative illustration where domain supports it** — Marx M1 delivery-worker example (unanimous Strong signal in Pass 1); does not pre-answer learner or worked-example item.

5. **Keep exposition distinct from task assignment** — teach concepts; assign writing in activity task / other materials.

6. **Do not append cognition-cue blocks to `text` bodies** — FM-07 on Photosynthesis M1/M2/M4; transfer, misconception, and revision prompts belong in designated material types or activity fields, not exposition slot.

7. **Avoid specification synopsis openings** — `This material explains…` not evidenced; teach directly.

8. **Avoid definition-table-only layout** — connective sentences or integrative paragraphs required; component glosses need relational closure (Photosynthesis key insight).

9. **Do not duplicate evaluate criteria exposition into definitional A1 text** — criteria teaching is separate material on evaluate arcs (Marx M12).

10. **Targeting unanimous Strong** — follow Marx M1: progression + relational links + applied example + clean body boundary. **Targeting minimum threshold** — Photosynthesis exposition block alone suffices per both passes; FM-07 still harms quality and Pass 1 Strong.

---

## Reviewer Guidance

Evaluate a `text` body against 44-2 §5.1:

1. **Failed check** — Synopsis only, empty body, task instruction dominant, or consolidation substituted for exposition (not on M1 pair).
2. **Minimum check** — Substantive prose; ≥2 linked ideas; HE tone; teaches not assigns (both M1).
3. **FM-07 check** — Cognition-cue or orientation metadata appended to exposition body; record separately from exposition scoring if following Inter-Rater split-body practice.
4. **FM-08 check (Pass 1 convention)** — Integrated applied illustration absent; note if applying Pass 1 Strong bar.
5. **Strong check** — Progression; relational explanation; `expected_output` alignment without spoiler; applied illustration (Marx); **declare Pass 1 vs Inter-Rater stance** on Photosynthesis-like bodies.
6. **Score exposition block** — If cognition cues appended, evaluate exposition paragraphs before appendage for instructional function; record FM-07 on full GAM body for capture quality.
