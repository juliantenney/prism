# Sprint 69 Goals

**Related:** [WHY-SPRINT-69.md](WHY-SPRINT-69.md) · [HANDOVER.md](HANDOVER.md) · [PLAN.md](PLAN.md)

---

1. Introduce a shared archetype grammar contract that preserves exact deterministic validation.
2. Keep producer ownership of canonical beat vocabulary and sequence legality.
3. Move exact validation from sequence enumeration to shared archetype grammar + deterministic mapping.
4. Preserve fail-closed diagnostics and exactly-once assignment guarantees.
5. Maintain certification readiness and browser/node parity.

## Non-goals

- Fuzzy matching
- nearest-sequence fallback
- ad hoc alias growth as primary strategy
- weakening validation or suppressing diagnostics
