## Executive summary

**CI and Marx fixtures already model disciplined transfer:** one-sentence `transfer_or_application_task` on the culminating activity plus a **named-move** `study_tips` bullet (*Where else would the procedure-vs-interval / purpose-and-difference move apply?*).

**Gaps:** **Climate** and **RNA** lack transfer or **limits-of-transfer** cues; some pages risk **over-broad** “real-world application” if prompts are vague. **37-5** adds a **Transfer and durable understanding** block that ties extension to **37-2 distinctions** and **37-4 closure** without duplicating epistemic synthesis bullets.

**Sprint 37 close-out:** Orientation → tension → progression → epistemic closure → **durable transfer** form a single rhetorical stack; this slice completes the chartered programme.

## Transfer vs extension vs comparison vs durable understanding

| Mode | What it does | Field / surface |
|------|--------------|-----------------|
| **Transfer** | Apply a **named move** in a **changed context** (one sentence) | `transfer_or_application_task`, one `study_tips` bullet |
| **Extension** | Adjacent setting, same discriminating rule | Scenario / new numbers with same move |
| **Comparison** | Judge two explanations/interpretations with **session criteria** | Debrief “which uses stronger evidence”; comparison tables |
| **Durable understanding** | Distinction remains usable after session | 37-4 “sustained” + 37-5 habit bullet |
| **Limit of transfer** | Where reasoning **fails** or assumptions break | “Recognise where… fails”; Marx assumption bullet |

**Do not conflate** transfer with closure (37-4): closure = what is clearer **now**; transfer = where the move applies **next** or **does not**.

- No `study_tips` bullet on applying evidence-classification to **new controversy** or **policy vs mechanism** limit.

### RNA fixture — **weak**

- MCQ completion without transferable diagnostic habit.
- **Target:** `study_tips` or `support_notes`: what discrimination should generalise to novel items vs what requires new evidence.

## Strong transfer patterns (keep)

1. **Named move + changed context (one sentence):** *Where else would comparing interval endpoints matter — name one context.*
2. **Assumption/limit sentence:** *Name one assumption in your factory explanation that might fail in a different workplace.*
3. **Comparison with session criteria:** Debrief — which explanation uses stronger evidence from the scenario?
4. **Durable habit bullet:** Tie to 37-2 distinction (procedure-vs-interval; purpose-vs-plot).
5. **Culminating activity** with less scaffolding (35-2) **plus** explicit transfer field — not recap prose.

---

## Failed transfer rhetoric (reject)

| Pattern | Why reject |
|---------|------------|
| “Apply to the real world” (unscoped) | No named move |
| “Continue exploring this topic” | Vague extension |
| Employability / career framing | Out of scope |
| Motivational future-use (“will help you in life”) | Non-epistemic |
| Over-broad transfer (“always use this table”) | Over-generalisation |
| Second recap paragraph after 37-4 closure | Dilutes synthesis |
| “Compare and contrast in general” | No session criteria |

---

## Over-generalisation risks

| Risk | Guard (37-5) |
|------|----------------|
| Move assumed universal | Require **one named context** where it applies |
| Policy from mechanism evidence | Climate: state when classification rules **do not** transfer to policy judgement |
| Statistical → causal overreach | CI: practical vs statistical significance limit |
| Plot summary as comparison | Marx: purpose-vs-plot guard on transfer |
| Memorising labels | RNA: interpret under **changed evidence**, not label recall |

---

## Relationship: closure (37-4) vs transfer (37-5)

| Layer | Question | Max length on page |
|-------|----------|-------------------|
| **37-4 epistemic synthesis** | What should now be clearer? | ≥1 bullet in `study_tips` |
| **37-5 transfer / durable** | Where does the move apply or fail? | ≤1 transfer + optional ≤1 limit bullet |
| **In-activity transfer** | Apply in new scenario | `transfer_or_application_task` on final activity |

Do not repeat the closure paragraph as transfer; do not add a third recap block.
