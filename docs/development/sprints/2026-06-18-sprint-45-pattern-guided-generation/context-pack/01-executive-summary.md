## What PRISM Is Doing Now

PRISM is a learning-design workflow producing self-directed higher-education learner resources through a chain of artefacts: learning content, knowledge model, outcomes, episode plans, learning activities, activity materials, learning sequence, and composed learner page.

**Sprint 43** settled educational architecture: investigation-primary ownership, resource-secondary voice, presence ≠ salience, supporting structures only.

**Sprint 44** made **instructional material quality** observable and reusable:

- Explicit **44-2 depth contracts** for eleven material types
- Frozen **benchmark corpus** (Marx, Photosynthesis)
- Three-pass **evaluation** with inter-rater validation
- **Pattern Library Draft 1** — six patterns, failure-mode registry, meta-principles MP-1–MP-8

**Sprint 45 (proposed)** would test whether those patterns can **influence GAM generation quality** without reopening architecture.
